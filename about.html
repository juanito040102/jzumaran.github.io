Juan Ignacio Zumarán Dubra is a graduate student pursuing a Master of Science in Data Analytics and Visualizations at Pratt. With a strong foundation in Python, Juan is passionate about leveraging machine learning and generative AI to enhance decision-making in various projects, including audio and text-to-speech models. He has a keen interest in statistical thinking and hypothesis testing. In addition to his academic pursuits, Juan has hands-on experience in information technologies, having developed tools for human resources information management in Uruguay and Mexico, and automating processes for major corporations like Merck.

I also love my friends and family (besides having a huge ego as you can imagine)
